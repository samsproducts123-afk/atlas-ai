How is Atlas different from ChatGPT?

Atlas lives in Telegram (no new app needed), responds 5-10x faster, remembers you permanently across sessions, includes 27+ built-in tools (image gen, crypto, PDF, expenses), and keeps your data 100% private on your own server. ChatGPT does none of this without paid plugins.

Is my data private?

100%. Atlas runs on a dedicated server - your conversations, files, and personal data never touch any cloud service. No data mining, no training on your data, no third-party access. It's your AI, running on your infrastructure.

What languages does Atlas support?

Atlas understands and responds in any language. Voice messages support 50+ languages including English, French, Spanish, German, Ukrainian, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, and many more.

Can I use Atlas for my business?

Absolutely. The Business plan includes multi-user support, custom personality, white-label options, and API access. Perfect for customer support, team productivity, or as a product feature.

How fast is Atlas?

Atlas responds in 1-2 seconds on average. That's 5-10x faster than ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. We use optimized inference servers with aggressive caching to achieve this speed.
